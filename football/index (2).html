About ScoutMe Gambia

ScoutMe Gambia is the premier platform for football talent discovery in The Gambia. We connect talented players with scouts and clubs, creating opportunities for career development and team building.

Our mission is to democratize football talent discovery, making it easier for players to showcase their skills and for scouts to find the next generation of football stars.
